Our Mission: Connecting Citizens and City Services

ResolveX was created to bridge the communication gap between citizens and local authorities. We provide a single, unified platform where public concerns—from broken streetlights to uncollected trash—can be quickly reported, tracked, and managed by dedicated staff and administrators. Our goal is to foster a proactive, collaborative environment for better urban living.
